(stack children & options)

Returns an entity based on Stack.

(stack [entity-1 entity-2])

Source
(defn stack*
  [children]
  (create-group (Stack.) children))
(defmacro stack
  [children & options]
  `(let [entity# (stack* ~children)]
     (u/calls! ^Stack (u/get-obj entity# :object) ~@options)
     entity#))
