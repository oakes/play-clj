(change-listener! screen k & options)

Calls a single method on the ChangeListener in the screen.

Options
:changed^ChangeListener.ChangeEvent event, ^Actor actor
:handle^Event event
Source
(defmacro change-listener!
  [screen k & options]
  `(let [listeners# (u/get-obj ~screen :ui-listeners)
         ^ChangeListener object# (u/get-obj listeners# :change-listener)]
     (u/call! object# ~k ~@options)))
